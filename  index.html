About HDS

Family owned.
Institutionally built.

Founded in 2002 by Henry V. Guindi in New York, HDS Trading Corp. started as a single-brand importing operation and grew into a full-stack housewares platform — with sourcing, design, compliance, logistics, and retail relationships all under one roof.

Today we operate as both a brand owner and a licensing partner — giving consumer brands direct access to 500+ retail relationships, a 3,800+ SKU product engine, and 30+ years of buyer relationships that cannot be manufactured overnight.

No middlemen.
No margin leakage.
No surprises.
